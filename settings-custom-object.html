Custom Objects

Extend your data model with objects specific to your business. Up to 25 custom objects per workspace.
What are custom objects?

Custom objects let you model data that doesn't fit into the standard CRM schema. They behave just like built-in objects — you can create records, define fields, link them to Contacts, Companies and Deals, and view them in the data model. Common examples include Opportunities, Projects, Contracts, Events, and Subscriptions.
